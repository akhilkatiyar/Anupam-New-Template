Our Story

Luxury Comfort Set Deep in Nature

We started with a simple dream: to create a place where people could unplug, breathe deeply, and feel at home in nature—without sacrificing comfort.Our journey began with weekend hikes, starry nights, and a deep appreciation for the peace that only the outdoors can offer. That passion grew into a mission to design a space that blends nature and nurture—a glamping experience that reconnects people to what matters.Every detail, from the linens to the lanterns, has been thoughtfully chosen to give you that just-right feeling of rustic luxury.
